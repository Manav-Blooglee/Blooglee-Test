Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of being a patron?

By being a patron you get many benefits such as special benefits on discord server like patron only giveaways, events etc. You also get a ticket every month and shout-outs at the end of articles.

What is a Ticket?

A Ticket is a thing you get every month by being a patron, by using it you can directly suggest us a topic for an article and we will make it, you will also be given the credits. To use the ticket, you need to message us at discord or patreon with details.

How many articles are posted every week?

Currently, we have a very small team hence we cannot post at much speed but we try to post two articles every week.

What is the privacy and advertisement policy?

The link to privacy policy is available in the footer of every page ,for the advertisement policy you may contact us.

How to join the discord server and get patron role?

The discord server is publicly available for anyone to join, you can get the link to join under every page but you get special features with patron role so to get the patron role you may contact us via discord, patreon or email with the details.
